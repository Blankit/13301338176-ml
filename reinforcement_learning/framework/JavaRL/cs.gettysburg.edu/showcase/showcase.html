Site Map
Information Resources maintains a campus-wide computing network. Through the network, students may access several programming languages and application packages. In addition, as a result if a grant from Sun Microsystems administered by Professor Tosten, Computer Science has its own state of the art Java-based laboratory. The laboratory contains 13 SunUltra5's. These machines are connected to the department's specialized parallel processing hardware. The Department also uses the Internet to access other sites for additional resources. These machines are used for some of the upper-division courses and for selected research topics. There are also several Sun SPARCstations and Ultra5's clustered in other locations throughout the departmental area for student use both during and after class hours.
