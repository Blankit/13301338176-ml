Site Map

 

    The Department of Computer Science offers a major and a minor in computer science. The Department believes that computer science is an interesting and lively subject that is an appropriate and accessible major and minor for all students, not just a select few. We are actively committed to developing a sense of community among students and faculty extending beyond the classroom. If you enjoy computer science and want to learn more, we invite you to look us over and then come study with us.
