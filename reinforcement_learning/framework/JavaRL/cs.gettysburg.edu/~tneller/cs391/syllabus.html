CS 391 - Selected Topics: Game Artificial Intelligence
Syllabus

Games of Tree Search

  • Breadth-first search, depth-first search, depth-limited search, iterative-deepening depth-first search (IDDFS)
  • Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) (e.g. Flat/Uniform, UCT)
  • RAVE Heuristic

Games of Chance

  • Reinforcement Learning and the n-Armed Bandit Problem
  • Temporal-Difference Learning

Games of Mixed Strategy

  • Regret Minimization
  • Counterfactual Regret Minimization (CFR)

Note: This syllabus is provided solely for overview purposes and is subject to change. It is not exhaustive and does not constitute a guarantee of what will or will not be covered in exams.
